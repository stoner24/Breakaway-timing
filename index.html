What We Do

Race Timing
Done Right

01

RFID Chip Timing

Impinj R420 RFID readers deliver sub-second accuracy for mass-start events. Every bib, every lap, every finish — captured automatically.

02

Live Results

Results publish to the web in real time as riders cross the line. Race directors, coaches, and spectators all get instant access.

03

Cyclocross Series

Official timing partner for the UTCX cyclocross series — Utah's premier cyclocross circuit with races across the Wasatch Front.

04

Mountain Bike Racing

From XC sprints to endurance events, we've timed races at Soldier Hollow, Corner Canyon, and venues across Utah's trail network.

05

Results Archive

Every race result is archived and accessible online. Athletes can look up past performances and race history anytime.

06

Event Consultation

Planning a new race? We help event directors set up timing systems, registration workflows, and results publishing from scratch.
