The Story

About Breakaway Timing

Breakaway Timing is a Utah-based race timing company founded by TJ Stone — a cyclist, racer, and timing technician with a passion for getting every split right.

We specialize in chip timing for cycling events across the Wasatch Front and beyond. Using Impinj R420 RFID readers and FinishLynx photo finish technology, we capture every bib crossing every line — whether it's the first wave off the line or the last finisher of the day.

Breakaway Timing is the official timing partner for the UTCX cyclocross series, one of Utah's most competitive and well-attended cycling circuits. We've also timed mountain bike races at Soldier Hollow, Corner Canyon, and venues across the state.

What sets us apart is a commitment to real-time results. Live data pushes to the web automatically as riders finish, so athletes, coaches, and spectators get instant access without waiting for manual exports or post-race spreadsheets.

Beyond timing, TJ races XC mountain bike competitively in Utah — so when we set up at a venue, we understand the course, the athlete experience, and what matters to the people crossing that line.

The Tech Stack

Equipment &
Technology

01

Impinj R420 RFID

Three Impinj R420 readers provide redundant coverage at start and finish lines. High-read-rate RFID captures every chip with precision timing accuracy.

02

FinishLynx

Industry-standard finish line timing software used at Olympic and world championship events. Produces the LIF files that feed our live results pipeline.

03

Live Results Pipeline

A custom Python system reads FinishLynx data in real time and pushes results to Firebase, making live standings available on the web within seconds of each finish.

04

Webscorer PRO

Webscorer provides registration management, wave starts, and announcer view integration for a complete race day workflow from sign-in to results.
